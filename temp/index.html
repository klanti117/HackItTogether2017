About Us

Brooklyn College, New York

As students at a large public university we have a firsthand understanding of the enormous expenses associated with being a college student. There are many students who do not have money for basic necessities such as food, clothing or the funds to cover the cost of books needed for their classes. On the other hand there are many students who have clothing they are seeking to dispose of and books they want to give away. There are also plenty of events on campus who end up having extra food, that often goes to waste. The Problem: how do we connect those in need to those who have items to give away. The Solution: Enter College Grabz!
